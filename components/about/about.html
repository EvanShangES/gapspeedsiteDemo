About Us

Leading Exhaust Industry

Golden Apple

Golden Apple, at first, was an exclusively owned Canadian company established in 1999, since then we have expanded and added factories and offices in China. At the very peak of an era that was outsourcing manufacturing processes from China, Golden Apple was one of the first few companies that were successful in this pursuit. Since then, Golden Apple has been supplying after-market auto parts for every type of vehicle, from compact cars to performance cars, to large suppliers and distributors from across the globe.

Over the years, the automotive manufacturing business has become more competitive, and our mission always has and always will be is to continuously produce quality products that will exceed the expectations of each and every one of our customers. Our new and improved team of sales staff and quality control officers will thoroughly check and oversee products before they are delivered.

Our workshop inside

Golden Apple process stainless and aluminized sheet coils into straight tubes that are cut to varying sizes. We utilize; automated CNC cold saws cut tubing machines, automated metal stamping machines, automatic or manual pipe bending systems, and seam welding techniques to create quality auto parts to deliver great power, sound, and performance.
